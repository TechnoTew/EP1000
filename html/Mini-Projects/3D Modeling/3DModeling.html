Knight Chess Piece
Requirements
  • Base: 30mm
  • Height: 50mm
  • Head Thickness: 5mm
  • Hollow Base

Firstly, I found a picture to serve as the base for the sketch. I first created a bounding box of 50mm height and 30mm width using a rectangle and scaled the image to fit snugly inside the box. This achieves the base and height requirement of this task

Secondly, I used the line, fillet, and 3 point arc tools to sketch out a 2d profile of the base of the chestpiece. And afterwards, I used the revolve tool to repeat the plane across the y axis to form the base of the chess piece.

Thirdly, for the head of the chess piece, I used the Fit Point Spline, 3 point arc, and lines to design it

Fourth, extruding the head of the chess piece to 5mm total
